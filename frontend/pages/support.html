Frequently Asked Questions

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout for 1-2 business day delivery. Custom PC builds may require an additional 2-3 business days for assembly and quality testing before shipping.

All PC MASTER pre-built systems come with a comprehensive 3-year warranty that covers parts and labor. This includes all internal components originally installed by PC MASTER. The warranty covers hardware failures and manufacturing defects but does not cover damage from accidents, misuse, or unauthorized modifications. Individual components purchased separately come with their manufacturer's warranty, typically 1-2 years.

To return a product, log into your account and go to your order history. Click on the order containing the item you wish to return and select "Start Return". You'll receive a prepaid shipping label via email. All returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery and products must be in original packaging with all accessories included. Custom-built PCs are subject to a 15% restocking fee unless they're being returned due to a defect covered under warranty.

If your PC is not performing as expected, first ensure all drivers are up to date. You can use our PC MASTER Driver Update Tool available for download in the support section. Also check that your system has proper ventilation and isn't overheating. If problems persist, contact our technical support team with your order number and a detailed description of the performance issues you're experiencing. Our technicians can run remote diagnostics or provide troubleshooting steps specific to your system configuration.

Yes, you can upgrade components in your pre-built PC. All PC MASTER systems are built using standard parts and form factors for maximum upgradeability. Upgrading components yourself will not void the warranty for other parts, though the new components will be covered by their manufacturer's warranty, not our system warranty. For assistance with choosing compatible upgrade parts or professional installation services, contact our technical support team.
